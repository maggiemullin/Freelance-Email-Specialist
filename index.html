About
Me

I'm Maggie — a freelance email marketing specialist and HTML email developer based in Collingwood, Ontario. I build emails from scratch: clean, hand-coded HTML that renders correctly across every major client, including the notoriously difficult Outlook desktop.

My background is in email marketing, where I've spent years not just designing campaigns but getting into the code — troubleshooting rendering bugs, building reusable template systems, and making sure what looks good in preview actually looks good in the inbox.

I specialize in HubSpot and work with small businesses, service providers, and growing brands who need emails that are both strategically sound and technically solid. No broken layouts, no clipped buttons, no guesswork.

Based remotely in Ontario and available to work with clients across Canada and the US.
